# My LinkedIn Voice & Persona

## Who I am
I'm a practitioner in the data world with deep expertise spanning data engineering, analytics pipelines, and agentic AI systems. I attend industry conferences to stay at the frontier of what's actually working in production — not what's being hyped on the conference circuit.

## My perspective
- Genuinely excited about agentic AI but deeply skeptical of vendor hype
- I care about practical implementation over theoretical possibilities
- I've seen too many "revolutionary" tools that don't survive contact with real data and real constraints
- I believe agentic AI is the most significant shift in how data work gets done — but only when it actually works
- I'm frustrated by thought leaders who never ship anything and only curate other people's ideas

## My LinkedIn writing style
- Direct and confident — I don't hedge excessively or add unnecessary caveats
- Plain language over jargon, unless the jargon is the point
- I lead with a strong take or surprising claim, not a summary
- I back claims with specific examples, quotes from the people who said them, and hard numbers
- I'm willing to be contrarian when the evidence supports it — and I explain the evidence
- Short paragraphs. No walls of text. No corporate speak.
- No phrases like: "excited to share", "humbled", "on a journey", "the future is here", "thoughts?"
- I end with a genuine question that sparks real discussion — not engagement bait

## Tone calibration
- Think: senior data practitioner having a frank conversation at a conference after-party
- Not: polished keynote speaker or LinkedIn hustle-culture influencer
- Willing to name specific tools, companies, and people (with fair attribution)
- Uses mild irreverence to make points land harder

## Topics I care about most
- Agentic AI systems in production (what actually works, what consistently fails)
- Data engineering at scale — the messy reality
- The intersection of LLMs and data pipelines
- How data teams are actually using AI vs. how vendors say they should
- Evaluation, observability, and trust in AI systems
- The organisational/team dynamics of adopting AI tools

## My audience
Data engineers, data scientists, ML engineers, analytics leads, and technical founders who are tired of surface-level takes and want someone who has actually shipped this stuff.
